Gyomei Himejima: Stone Hashira

❝ Ahh… What a shoddy child she is. How sorrowful. It’s so sorrowful that she was born into this world. ❞

Gyomei Himejima is a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the current Stone Hashira. Gyomei also serves as the mentor of Genya Shinazugawa.

Gyomei Himejima is part of the Demon Slayer Corps and is the Stone Pillar in the Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon Slayer) series. He uses the Breath of Stone (岩の呼吸 Iwa no Kokyu) and is considered the strongest out of the Pillars. He is also the tallest out of all the Pillars. Gyomei has a long scar running horizontally across his forehead caused by demons and no pupils since he is blind. It is unknown whether he was born blind or if an accident caused it. He has short black hair and wears the standard Demon Slayer's uniform as well as a green haori with kanji’s holding religious meanings written all over. He always wears a large beaded necklace around his neck and is constantly seen holding prayer beads whenever he isn't engaging in battle.

Appearance

Gyomei is a hulking figure among the Demon Slayers. He is one of the tallest characters in the series, easily towering over his fellow Hashira. He is powerfully built and extremely muscular. He has spiky black hair and a prominent scar running horizontally across his forehead. Having been blind since childhood, he has white eyes with no visible irises or pupils. His eyes frequently tear-up when he gets emotional.

Gyomei wears a black and white uniform typical of a Demon Slayer, with bandages over his shins resembling kyahan and white zōri with an olive green happi draped over his shoulders. The collared frame of the happi contains the kanji for the Japanese Nianfo/Shin Buddha chant (南な無む阿あ弥み陀だ仏ぶつ Namu Amida Butsu?) from the top-down, along with the separate kanji all over at the back. He also sports a large-beaded necklace around his neck and carries a set of ojuzu beads when he is not engaged in battle.

He sustains a scar across his forehead after being slashed by a crescent moon blade from Kokushibo. His left leg from the knee below was later severed during the battle against Muzan.

Personality

Gyomei is very religious, often seen offering prayers to people and situations which sadden him. He is gentle and highly emotional, frequently tearing up in all sorts of situations. Despite him being kind and emotional he is very objective and doubtful of people especially children, thinking of them as pure, naive, weak, and completely fine with cruelty. However, once he accepts someone, no matter what others will say he will not doubt them. This is seen when he fully accepts Tanjiro Kamado and his sister, Nezuko who he thought was better executed.

Abilities

  • Master Swordsman: Within the Demon Slayer Corps, Gyomei is considered strongest out of all the pillars. He is also a skilled swordsmen.

  • Unique Weapon: Gyomei wields a hand axe complete with a spiked flail attached to the hilt through a long chain. To wield his unique weapon he uses the sound of the chain to judge the position of the flail and axe, covering for and even taking advantage of his blindness. The chain, spike flail, and axe are all created using the same metals used in the creation of Nichirin Blades. The craftsmanship of the weapon is noted to be incredibly high class and even superior to the swords forged during the Sengoku era, where smithing and sword forging was already at its peak.

  • Enhanced Strength: Gyomei possesses immense physical strength, shown when he uses his strength to push a large boulder bigger than him through a town. Through ‘Repetitive Actions’, he is capable of doing a set of predetermined movements in order to maximize concentration.

  • Enhanced Reflexes: During a battle with Kokushibo, Gyomei was complimented on his extremely agile, fast and quick reflexes allowing him to dodge attack easy, when in contrast to Gyomei's large build.

  • Enhanced Hearing: Gyomei has a heightened sense of hearing, which enables him to hear the sound of Muzan Kibutsuji's flesh regenerating.

  • See Through World (透き通る世界 Sukitoru Sekai): A different perspective of the world. Despite being blind, Gyomei gained the ability to see without using his eyes. Through naturally sensing the people and objects around him, Gyomei can create images of his enemies in his mind.

  • Demon Slayer Mark: During his battle against the Upper Moon One, Kokushibo, Gyomei awakened his Demon Slayer Mark. It appears as a cracked earth like patterns on both of his forearms. When activated his physical speed, strength, and reflexes increased dramatically.


Total Concentration Breathing: Breath of Stone

  • First Form: Serpentinite Bipolar - The user throws both the flail and axe towards their intended target and manipulates the chain by rotating it thus causing the axe and flail to also rotate in order to drill and grind right through their target.

  • Second Form: Upper Smash - The user throws both their axe and spiked flail for a pincer move before smashing down on the chain to cause the flail to then rebound and smash into their target from afar.

  • Third Form: Stone Skin - The user rapidly twirls and swings the chain around them to unleash an omnidirectional flurry of attacks with both the flail and axe. This technique can also be used to block and counter enemy attacks from all directions at once.

  • Fourth Form: Volcanic Rock, Rapid Conquest - The user manipulates both the flail and axe weapons at both ends of the chain by swinging the chain on both sides in order to strike their opponents using a two pronged wide ranged attack.

  • Fifth Form: Arcs of Justice - The user utilizes a powerful barrage with their flail and axe, doing a great amount of damage. The axe is destined to hit first with the trajectory of the flail following from the opposite side and slamming it into the opponent.
