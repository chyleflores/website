Sanemi Shinazugawa: Wind Hashira

❝ I know how unfair the world is. . . because good people. . . die one after another. ❞

Sanemi Shinazugawa is a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the current Wind Hashira.

Sanemi Shinazugawa is part of the Demon Slayer Corps in the Kimetsu no Yaiba (Demon Slayer) series. He uses the Breath of Wind and is the Wind Pillar. Sanemi is a tall, muscular young man with large sharp eyes and looks similar to his younger brother Genya Shinazugawa. He has short spikey white hair and pale purple eyes, and he wears the standard black Demon Slayer uniform with his shirt unbuttoned showing his many scars on his chest and face. He wears a short white haori cropped to above his hips with the kanji for ‘kill’ written on the back.

Appearance

Sanemi is a tall man of very muscular build, with spiky long white hair and large almond-shaped eyes with purple irises. His face and body are covered with scars, accrued over his many years of fighting demons. He cuts his hair short somewhere in between the events of the Hashira meeting and the Mugen Train incidnet. He is usually seen wearing a crazed expression on his face.

He wears a black standard Demon Slayer uniform, tinted dark green in the anime, unbuttoned to expose his chest and abs with a white long sleeved haori over it with the kanji for kill (殺さつ satsu?) etched on the back, white buckles around his shins and tabi socks with a pair of white zōri with green straps.

Personality

Sanemi is very hot blooded, short tempered, and stubborn. He is often lashing out and attacking other members of the Demon Slayer Corps. The only person he shows respect to and speaks respectfully to is Kagaya Ubuyashiki the 97th leader of the Demon Slayer Corps only after realising the man was much more than what his sickly appearance seemed. Sanemi's cold and rude behavior is due to him losing his family he deeply cared about in the past. When his brother Genya Shinazugawa joins the Demon Slayer Corps he insults and verbally abuses him and tries to force Genya Shinazugawa to quit the organisation so he wouldn't be in any danger. He had wanted his brother he loved so much to live a happy life with a wife and many children without worrying about Demons.

Abilities

  • Master Swordsman: As part of the Demon Slayer Corps and being a Pillar, Sanemi is a highly ranked and skilled swordsmen. So skilled that the Demon, Kokushibo who has hundreds of years of experience is impressed by him.

  • Intoxicant Blood (稀血 Marechi): Sanemi has a rare type of blood which was later revealed to be one of that is incredibly intoxicating to Demons. It is also unique due to its intense potency that the smallest whiff of his blood immediately causes demons to salivate, lose focus and go crazy for it. The only Demon immune to this is Nezuko, due to her stern rule of not hurting humans.

  • Muscle Control: Sanemi has shown the ability to control his muscles more directly compared to the average human. Sanemi can manipulate muscle tissue to close wounds, preventing bleeding out if hurt in battle. This is shown in the fight with Kokushibo when he was cut across the stomach.

  • Demon Slayer Mark: During battle against Upper Moon One, Kokushibo, Sanemi awakened his Demon Slayer Mark which takes the appearance of a single paper origami windmill with two dots on both sides on his right cheek. When this manifested, Sanemi's physical speed, strength, and reflexes increased dramatically.
